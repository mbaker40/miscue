# P1 — Route pre-generation (`src/route.ts`) + node events (`src/events.ts`)

**Files you own:** `src/route.ts` (new), `src/events.ts` (new). Touch nothing else.
**Read first:** `docs/tasks/README.md` (contracts, tags, invariants), `src/rng.ts`, `src/upgrades.ts`.

## Goal / dossier clauses

D5: *"After most tables the player picks the next node from 2–3 visible options (Rack · Shop/Re-Chalk ·
Forge · Money Table · Rest · Mystery). … show a short forward fan (next 2–3 choices) … Space the power
jumps (shops) so one lands roughly every 3–4 tables."*
D4: *"Forge = crack yourself on purpose to deepen an upgrade (a glass-cannon archetype falls out naturally)."*

The forward fan requires the whole route's *offered options* to exist at run start — so the route is generated
once from the seed, in its own RNG stream, independent of what the player later picks.

## `src/route.ts`

Implement exactly the types, `actOf`, tag constants, and `generateRoute` from the contract in
`docs/tasks/README.md` (copy the tag constants verbatim — they are frozen).

`generateRoute(seed)` rules:
- Returns exactly 12 `RouteSlot`s; `slot.depth = i + 1`; `slot.act = actOf(depth)`.
- Uses only `new Rng(seed ^ ROUTE_TAG)` (import `Rng` from `./rng`) — never `Math.random()`.
- Fixed slots (single option each):
  - depth 1: `{ kind: 'rack', title: 'First rack', desc: 'The underhall opens. Clear it, get paid, re-forge.' }`
  - depth 4: `{ kind: 'miniboss', title: 'The Rack-Master', desc: 'Act I ends here. It wants to see your build.', cls: 'pink' }`
  - depth 12: `{ kind: 'boss', title: 'The bottom of the run', desc: 'Something black is waiting at the last table.', cls: 'pink' }`
- Every other slot: `options[0]` is always the rack option (invariant #4), then **1–2 side options**
  (`rng.next() < 0.55` → 2, else 1), drawn without replacement from the side bag below.
- **Shop guarantee:** at depths 3, 7, 11 the FIRST side option is always `shop`; remaining side slots (if any)
  draw from the bag excluding shop.
- Side bag (equal weights, drawn via `rng.pick` on the remaining candidates): `shop`, `rest`, `money`,
  `forge`, `mystery`. No duplicate kinds within one slot.

## `src/events.ts`

Imports: `Rng` from `./rng`, `PlayerStats` from `./upgrades`.

**Forge pool** — deepenings are repeatable across visits (stacking multipliers on a near-shatter ball is the
glass-cannon archetype). `forgeOptions(rng, n = 2)` returns `n` DISTINCT picks from:

| id | label | desc | apply |
|---|---|---|---|
| f-power | `Overstrike` | `Hit 20% harder than a soul should.` | `s.maxPower *= 1.2` |
| f-slow | `Slow Blood` | `Deep time drains 25% slower.` | `s.strokeDrain *= 0.75` |
| f-eyes | `Sharp Eyes` | `Style pays out 30% bigger Stroke.` | `s.strokeGain *= 1.3` |
| f-bent | `Bent True` | `Your english bends 35% deeper.` | `s.curve *= 1.35` |
| f-dense | `Dense Core` | `A quarter again your mass. You hit like a grudge.` | `s.mass *= 1.25` |

**Mystery** — `rollMystery(rng, ctx)` picks one outcome. Roll `r = rng.next()`:
- `r < 0.30` → `{ title: 'A stash under the rail', desc: 'Somebody hid chalk here and never came back for it.', chalk: 8 }`
- `r < 0.55` → if `ctx.cracks > 0`: `{ title: 'A kind stranger', desc: 'Gone before you can ask a name. One crack polished smooth.', repair: 1 }`
  else fall through to the stash outcome with `chalk: 4` and desc `'Nothing needs mending. You pocket the tip instead.'`
- `r < 0.80` → `{ title: 'A long drink of quiet', desc: 'The felt hums low. Your Stroke settles full.', strokeFill: true }`
- else → `{ title: 'Hustled', desc: 'You never even saw the cue move. Your chalk is lighter.', chalk: -Math.min(5, ctx.chalk) }`

Mystery must NEVER add cracks (invariant #1) and never drops chalk below 0 (the clamp above).

## Out of scope

No edits to `game.ts`, `hud.ts`, `waves.ts`, or `rng.ts`. No rendering, no DOM. Do not consume from any RNG
other than the one passed in / derived from `ROUTE_TAG`.

## Acceptance

1. `npx tsc --noEmit` passes.
2. Bundle + assert (see README "standalone logic assertions"):
   - `generateRoute(123).length === 12`; two calls with seed 123 are `JSON`-identical (determinism).
   - Slots at depths 1, 4, 12 each have exactly one option, of kinds `rack`/`miniboss`/`boss`.
   - Depths 3, 7, 11: some option has `kind === 'shop'`.
   - Every non-fixed slot: `options[0].kind === 'rack'`, 2–3 options total, no duplicate kinds.
   - `forgeOptions(new Rng(1), 2)` → 2 options with distinct ids; applying `f-power` to `baseStats()` gives
     `maxPower === 1.2`.
   - `rollMystery` over seeds 1..200: no result with negative post-clamp chalk given `ctx.chalk = 3`; `repair`
     only ever appears with `ctx.cracks > 0`.
